Why Say NO to Drugs

Drug abuse destroys lives and communities. Saying NO to drugs means choosing a path of health, opportunity, and freedom. Every time you say NO, you're saying YES to a better future.

  • Drugs damage your physical and mental health
  • Addiction destroys relationships and opportunities
  • Drug abuse leads to legal problems and financial ruin
  • Communities suffer from increased crime and violence
  • Recovery is possible, but prevention is better

Contact & Help

If you or someone you know needs help, or if you want to report drug-related activity, please use the contacts below. Your action can save lives!

Police Emergency

Call 100 (India) or your local emergency number to report drug activity or emergencies.

National Anti-Drug Helpline

Call 1800-11-0031 (India) for counseling, support, and information.
